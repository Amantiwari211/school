About Our School

T.R CONVENT was established in 1994 and it is managed by the Pvt. Unaided. It is located in Rural area. It is located in DEO block of AURANGABAD (BIHAR) district of Bihar. The school consists of Grades from 1 to 8. The school is Co-educational and it have an attached pre-primary section. The school is N/A in nature and is not using school building as a shift-school. English is the medium of instructions in this school. This school is approachable by all weather road. In this school academic session starts in April.

Interactive Classrooms

Our classrooms are designed to foster creativity and learning. Equipped with smart boards, comfortable seating, and well-ventilated space, they provide the perfect environment for academic excellence and student engagement.
